About us

Our Mission

This website was created based on a Newcastle University Project. Our mission was to create a web page available to everyone so that they can report any issues they may face in their everyday life. Our goal was to make sure that awareness of those issues will be spread so that everyone can be informed about them and be able to avoid them. That could involve closed roads, malfunctioning traffic lights or lights and many more issues that can appear in a city's infrastructure.
